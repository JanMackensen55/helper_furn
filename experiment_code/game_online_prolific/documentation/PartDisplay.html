PartDisplay

costsOne

The costs of the first part of the item. It is used to display the costs of the part in the user interface. If the option PRODUCE_ONE_CLICK is activated, the costs are scaled to represent the total costs of producing the amount of parts to built the final item. As an example: If the part costs 1 metal but the part is required 4 time to produce the item, its costs will be displayed as 4 metal.
